How to Add/Edit Reminders

Adding a Remider

There are 2 ways to add a Reminder.

  • Clicking on the Add Reminder toolbar icon
  • Selecting "Add" from the Reminder menu.

Editing a Reminder

There are 2 ways to edit a Reminder.

  • Clicking on the Edit Reminder toolbar icon
  • Selecting "Edit" from the Reminder menu.

Configuring the Reminder

Once you have choosen to Add or Edit a Reminder you will be
presented with a "sheet" that will drop down over the Reminder Window.

This Reminder configuration sheet will look like the following:

You will then be able to fill out the following fields:

  • Reminder Title: This is the Title/Subject of the Reminder.

  • Reminder Message: This is the Message/Body of the Reminder.

  • Remind me at: Selecting this option will let you enter a time of day to be reminded.
    You should enter a standard AM/PM time here.

  • Remind me in: Selecting this option will let you enter a Reminder that goes off
    within a certain amount of time from the present. (i.e. 5 minutes from now)

  • Play Sound: Checking this box will let you choose what sound to play
    and how many times when the Reminder goes off.

  • Repeat Reminder: Checking this box will repeat the Reminder continously.

  • Save Reminder: Checking this will let you save a Reminder
    (so that it can endure through the dark time when EggTimer isn't running).
    EggTimer will let you know if the Reminder is due or not the next time it starts up.
    The Reminder will automatically appear in the Reminder Window the next time EggTimer is running.
