What does the Reminder Alert Panel look like?

Reminder Alert Panel

The Reminder Alert Panel is the way that EggTimer lets you know that a reminder is due.

Here is what it would typically look like.

You are given three options when the Alert Panel shows up.

  • You can click Okay. If the Reminder is a repeating one it will keep repeating.
  • You can click Expire. This will manually expire the Reminder.
  • You can click Delete. This will delete the Reminder permanently.
